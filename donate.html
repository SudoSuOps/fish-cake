Why fishcake

In a family of ten children, you learn to stretch what you have. You don't throw food away. You make fishcakes — cheap protein that feeds everyone at the table.

We do the same thing with compute. A corporation retires a workstation that still has years of life. A data center decommissions a GPU that can still run AI models. Instead of landfill, it becomes a kid's first dev rig.

Every child who wants to build should have a machine to build on. Not a phone. Not a tablet. A real computer with a terminal, a text editor, and the ability to run code.

That's fishcake. Stretch compute. Feed everyone.

Donate hardware

Got old computers, laptops, GPUs, or servers? We'll take anything that powers on. We handle wiping, refurbishing, and shipping to kids.

  • Desktop computers (any age, any brand)
  • Laptops (working screen + keyboard)
  • GPUs (any NVIDIA card with 2GB+ VRAM)
  • Monitors, keyboards, mice
  • Servers, networking equipment
  • Edge devices (Raspberry Pi, Jetson, NUC)

We'll arrange pickup for large donations (10+ units). For smaller donations, we provide a prepaid shipping label. All shipping is always free — donations and purchases.
